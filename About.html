About Us

Our name and vision is derived from our founder's daughter - Joyce Leslie. We have been in business for over 65 years. Joyce Leslie was born in Brooklyn in 1945 and grew up in the rag business where she listened and learned. Father knows best and at an early age he began to groom his first born; teaching her the importance of always providing our customer tremendous value. Joyce Leslie was a natural. She was born with an instinctive flair for style that has been nurtured and honed through the years, making it possible to bring you a fabulous selection of the season's hottest trends.

Count on Joyce Leslie to have the best styles for any occasion - whether it's school, work to play, a hot date, girls' night out or club-hopping. Now, this retail chain is proud to be available online so you never have to leave your seat to have the most up-to-date fashions you desire. Joyce Leslie makes it easy for all who are style-obsessed to shop savvy and sassy with just the click of a button! Over the years, our namesake has consistently traveled the world to provide a dynamic shopping experience for her loyal customer base. The always trendy and super chic Joyce Leslie is the ultimate consumer, shopper and fashion lover. Her name has become synonymous with the quality, trendy and edgy styles we bring to the thrifty fashionista. We cater to the gals that have champagne taste... without the celebrity income!

Our customer base transcends the age demographic, spanning everyone from hip and hot gals to trendy and modern moms. The criteria for a Joyce Leslie girl are an urban attitude, contemporary vibe and fierce fashion sense. At Joyce Leslie you'll always find daily deals on dresses, tops, denim, swimwear, handbags, jewelry, shoes, accessories and more. We carry the hottest looks from Jersey Girl glam to West Coast chic!
